Minetoons

My artistic take on my favorite videogame

If I had to pick one game as my absolute favorite, it will have to be Minecraft.
This game caught my attention as an eleven year old kid, and still keeps me invested in it to this day.
Saying this game is something special for me would be an understatement. As I have made my strongest friendships while playing this game. And that, is the core of these toons I create every now and then.
Recreating my friends and some of our adventures in the style of Minecraft is one of my favorite things to do regarding drawing toons.
