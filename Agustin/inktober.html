InkTober

Art Event

Every year, the month of october fills all social media with great art, with the purpose of giving people the challenge of doing a drawing on ink each day of the month. And since 2018, it is a month I enthusiastically look up to.
But what I love the most about Inktober is that I can experiment with a different style than my usual cartoonish, cutsie characters. During InkTober I adopt a creepier, more sinister style, which at times can also have cute elements in a darker tone.
It is just a ton of fun to do each year!
