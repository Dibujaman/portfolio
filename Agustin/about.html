Agustin Guerrero

Digital Artist

Malmö, Sweden

Born and raised in Uruguay, I practiced and trained my drawing skill in both the digital and the traditional pen and paper techniques. Having worked as a freelance digital artist since the age of 16 gave me experience in customer service and improved my art skills significantly.
By the age of 18, having recently finished my high school studies, I decided to move to Sweden and then start my university studies. At the age of 20 years I decided to seek for work experiences to enrich my knowledge and skill in different fields while studying for my bachelor studies. Currently I continue to freelance while studying in small projects around the world.
