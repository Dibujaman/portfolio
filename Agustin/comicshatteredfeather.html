Shattered Feather

Short Comic

This all started as the final project for my "Digital Comics" class in university in 2018. We were expected to make a first episode to a story of our own. And so, Shattered feather came to be.
Years after having completed that first episode I kept thinking about the story, which may one day get somewhere. Yet I did keep on working on the main character, Katherine.
